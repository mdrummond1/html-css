Dirt Cake Recipe

Ever since I was young, I’ve had a love for pudding. I LOVE PUDDING! This recipe is one that I begged my mom to make for my birthday every year. I am fairly certain that this was my birthday of choice for at least 5 years in a row, if not more. We always used chocolate pudding, but you could easily substitute vanilla if you prefer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pkg. of oreos
  • 1 pkg. of cream cheese
  • 4 Tbsp. butter softened
  • 1 c. powdered sugar
  • 2 pkg (3.4 oz) instant chocolate pudding
  • 3.5 c. cold milk
  • 1 carton (12 oz) whipped topping

Steps:

  1. In a food processor, or blender, crush the cookies until fine and set aside. (If you would like to snack on a few you may, but not too many)
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. In a third bowl mix pudding and milk until well blended
  4. Fold the pudding into the cream cheese mixture
  5. Fold in the whipped cream
  6. In a large serving bowl start with the filling and alternate layers with the cookie crumbs ending with crumbs on top
  7. Chill for a few hours or overnight
  8. Optional: For a fun twist you can place gummi worms on top to givea more dirt-like feel.
