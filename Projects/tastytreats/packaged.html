Millville Crunchy Peanut Butter Granola Bar

Sometimes all you need is a quick snack to give you energy and fill you up between meals. My favorite snack is a good peanut granola bar. I love peanut butter with a passion! This snack gets me my pb fix as well as fills me up until dinner time.

Why this one?

This bar is packed with so much peanut butter flavor! These bars are also pretty healthy. You could eaten a bag of chips, but these are a much healthier option. They are also quick. There is no preparation needed, you just open a package and eat up. You can take them anywhere to eat on the go. I keep 4 or 5 packages in my backpack to snack on throughout my week of classes.
